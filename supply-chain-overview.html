A two-phase supply chain

PHASE 1 Load Plan (LP)
PUSH Model
Ship everything out as it arrives in Dallas. Don't wait — push it to the 8 regional and satellite warehouses as fast as possible. Clear Dallas warehouse space. Time is the enemy.
PHASE 2 Last Mile (LM)
PULL Model
Deliver based on venue bump-in dates. Each venue "pulls" its delivery when it is required. Destination warehouse to venue, on schedule.
